Getting Help

You may access the IFTDSS this Help Center anytime by clicking the Help icon in the top right of the menu bar.

Help Center - will take you to the Help Center main page and search bar.

About this Page - will take you to the specific topic that corresponds to the page your on within IFTDSS.

As you proceed through the help you will notice Notes, Tips, and Important information

text marked 'important' indicates information you need to know for the run or operation to successfully complete IFTDSS.

 

a note includes helpful information that applies to the task you are performing.

 

a tip includes information that will save time, or make subsequent processes in IFTDSS easier

 

text here describes features that will be added to IFTDSS in the near future
