About the Planning Cycle

 

What is the Planning Cycle?

The Planning Cycle lets you navigate through IFTDSS to perform an ordered series of steps for different stages of landscape planning. Selecting any stage of the Planning Cycle will bring up a series of ordered tasks associated with that stage. For example, by selecting the Landscape Evaluation stage, you are presented with options to create landscape, evaluate and edit landscape features (such as fuel models) for accuracy, and run basic models to view fire behavior and prioritize areas on the landscape for treatment.

The stages of the Planning Cycle include:

Landscape Evaluation
  • Generate a Landscape
  • Edit or Correct a Landscape
  • Calculate Landscape Fire Behavior
  • Calculate a Landscape Burn Probability
  • Develop Project Objectives
