Using Your Own Data

One of the benefits that makes IFTDSS so convenient in the fuels planning process is the ability to upload your own data to the cloud, to populate models, forms, reports, and access from any computer.

Here is a list of the types of data you may import into IFTDSS, and the requirements to upload each one:

Shapefiles

Shapefiles are used in IFTDSS for indicating treatment areas, and also creating masks that you can use in geospatial analysis.

Shapefiles are uploaded via the Map Studio Add Shapefile button.

I want details about shapefile upload requirements.

Landscape File (LCP) uploading will be available during the next (Summer 2017) release of IFTDSS, so stay tuned!
